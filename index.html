Our Services

We are here to help make your ideas come to life! We are located in the printer lab, dedicated to helping creative minds create 3d print jobs.

Designs

Head to www.thingiverse.com to find premade 3d print jobs.

Custom Designs

If any patron has a custom made print job that they would like to print, please bring the usb to our volunteers.

Volunteer Application

If a patron would like to began volunteering for the 3d print lab, then fill out this form and turn it into the front desk.

Donations

The 3d print lab will always take donations from patrons in order to support the maker lab.
